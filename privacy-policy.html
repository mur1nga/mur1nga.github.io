Privacy Policy

At AnaesWise, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our app and services.

Information We Collect

AnaesWise uses authentication methods, which may require the collection of personal information. We offer the following sign-in options:

  • Email and password authentication
  • Google sign-in
  • Apple sign-in
  • Anonymous sign-in (no personal data collected)

When you sign in with your email, Google, or Apple, we may collect and store personal information such as your email address and any other data provided by the sign-in service. No additional information is collected through anonymous sign-in.

Data Usage and Storage

The information collected is used to provide access to app features, maintain user sessions, and personalize your experience. Your data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties, except for the following cases:

  • Authentication services (e.g., Firebase Authentication for email, Google, Apple sign-in)
  • In cases where we are legally obligated to disclose user data

Third-Party Services

AnaesWise uses third-party services such as Firebase for authentication and analytics. These services may collect certain data as part of their processes (e.g., device information, login times). For more information on how Firebase handles your data, please review their privacy policy at Firebase Privacy Policy.
